I am a Robotics and Mechanical Engineer passionate about designing and building human-centered robotic systems. I have experience in designing and fabricating soft robotic actuators, wearable systems, and bio-inspired devices, and I enjoy bridging mechanical design and robotics to create devices that interact safely with people.

I recently earned my M.S. in Robotics and Autonomous Systems from Boston University. My B.S. was in Mechanical Engineering at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI) with a minor in Studio Arts, and was also a member of the RPI Varsity Swim Team, specializing in sprint events.
