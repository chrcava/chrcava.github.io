I am a Robotics and Mechanical Engineer passionate about developing human-centered robotic systems. My experience spans the design and fabrication of soft robotic actuators, wearable systems, and bio-inspired mechanisms. I earned my M.S. in Robotics and Autonomous Systems from Boston University. I have a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Studio Arts from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI), where I was also a member of the Varsity Swim Team specializing in sprint events.
